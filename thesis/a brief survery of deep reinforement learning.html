1.2 Introduction

The most important property of deep learning is that deep neural networks can automatically find compact low-dimensional representations (features) of high-dimensional data (e.g., images, text and audio).

Although this assumption is held by the majority of RL algorithms, it is somewhat unrealistic, as it requires the states to be fully observable. 马尔科夫性 —— 当前状态只由上个状态决定，在 RL 的算法中是默认满足的。但这其实有点不切实际，因为它需要当前的状态是完全可观察的

This means that Q can be improved by bootstrapping, i.e., we can use the current values of our estimate of Q π to improve our estimate. Q 值可以通过 bootstrapping 更新，即：可以利用下一状态的预测的 Q 值更新之前状态预测的 Q 值。
