## Definition {#definition}

A topological order of a [Directed Acyclic Graphs]({{< relref "20231030175630-directed_acyclic_graphs.md" >}}) is an ordering of its nodes so that for every edge \\((v\_i, v\_j)\\) we have \\(i < j\\)
